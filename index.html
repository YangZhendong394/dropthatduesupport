How to Get Help

Q: Why can't I see notification alerts?

Please ensure that notification permissions are enabled for Drop That Due in iOS Settings (Settings → Notifications → Drop That Due → Allow Notifications). Also, notifications are only triggered on the due date or the advance reminder day.

Q: How do I add items to the system calendar?

On the add or edit item page, tap the "Add to Calendar" button. Calendar events are set as all-day events by default, and the reminder time is automatically calculated based on the advance days you set.

Q: Does the app require internet?

Not at all. Drop That Due runs 100% offline, and all data is stored only on your device with no information uploaded.

Q: Which languages are supported?

Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese, English, Japanese, Korean, and Spanish, automatically following the system language.
